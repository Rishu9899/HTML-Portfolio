The Best Movies According to me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

  1. Iris

    2. True story of the lifelong romance between novelist Iris Murdoch and her husband John Bayley, from their student days through her battle with Alzheimer's disease.

  2. The Departed

    3. An undercover cop and a mole in the police attempt to identify each other while infiltrating an Irish gang in South Boston

  3. The Matrix

    4. When a beautiful stranger leads computer hacker Neo to a forbidding underworld, he discovers the shocking truth--the life he knows is the elaborate deception of an evil cyber-intelligence.
